Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Krishak?
klrishak is an advanced agriculture app that helps farmers optimize crop production, access market trends, and receive expert guidance.
2. Who can use this app?
This app is built for farmers, agribusiness professionals, researchers, and anyone looking to improve agricultural productivity.
3. What features does the app offer?
- Smart Crop Management
- Weather Forecasts & Alerts
- Real-time Market Prices
- Pest & Disease Control Advice
- Community Support & Discussion Forums
- Soil Testing & Analysis
- AI-Based Yield Predictions
- Organic Farming Guidelines
- Smart Irrigation Insights
4. Is the app available in multiple languages?
Yes! Krishak supports multiple languages to help farmers across different regions.
5. Does the app require an internet connection?
Some features like market trends and weather updates require the internet, but basic functionalities work offline.
6. What type of crops does this app support?
The app supports a variety of crops, including grains, fruits, vegetables, and cash crops.
7. Is my personal data safe?
Yes! We prioritize security and ensure that your data is protected through encryption and privacy safeguards.
8. Can I get real-time pest and disease alerts?
Yes, the app provides automated alerts for pest infestations and crop diseases based on your region and crop type.
9. Does the app offer guidance on organic farming?
Absolutely! We provide expert recommendations and best practices for organic farming to boost sustainability.
10. Can I connect with other farmers?
Yes! Our built-in farmer community lets you discuss best practices and exchange ideas with fellow agricultural experts.
